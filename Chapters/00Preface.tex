As this thesis continues the work of my preceding thesis "Processor-level Accleration of Tsetlin Machines Using Custom RISC-V ISA Extensions" in the subject TFE4595, parts of this thesis are based on or taken from this thesis. Parts of Chapter \ref{chap:theory} are based on the same chapter in the project thesis, and same with parts of Chapter \ref{chap:method}, however to a lesser extent. As a large point of this thesis is to investigate how the project thesis solution scales, CFU Design 1 (\ref{sec:cfu_design1}) is very similar to the solution from the project thesis. The section covering NoisyXOR (part Section \ref{sec:other_datasets}) has been copied directly from Section 3.1 from the project thesis.

Though some of the topics covered are similar, almost nothing has been reused in its entirety, instead being rewritten and expanded upon.
